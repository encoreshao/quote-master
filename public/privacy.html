Information We Do Not Collect

Nexus Tab respects your privacy and is designed with this principle in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from our users. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Personal Information — We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • Usage Data — We do not track or store information about your use of the Service, including your interactions with bookmarks, tasks, or widgets.
  • Device Information — We do not collect information about your device, such as IP address, browser type, or operating system.

How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any personal or usage information, we do not use your information in any way.

All features of Nexus Tab operate locally on your device. Data related to bookmarks, tasks, notes, quick links, and settings is stored locally using Chrome's built-in storage API (chrome.storage.local). Nothing leaves your browser.

Third-Party Services

Some optional widgets may connect to external services only when configured by you. These connections are initiated by your explicit action — no data is sent to our servers.

Weather Widget
Connects to OpenWeather API when you provide an API key and city
RSS Feeds
Fetches articles from feed URLs you configure
GitLab / GitHub
Connects to respective APIs when you provide access tokens
Background Image
Loads an image from a URL you set for the backdrop
AI Assistant
Sends your prompts to the AI provider you choose (OpenAI, Claude, or Gemini) when you use the assistant. Your API key is stored locally; we do not see your requests.

Security

Although we do not collect any data, we prioritize the security of information stored locally on your device. Nexus Tab employs industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

All data is stored within Chrome's sandboxed extension storage, which is isolated from other extensions and websites.

Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any information, we also do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. If we make material changes, we will notify you by updating the date at the top of this policy.
